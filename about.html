Our Mission

Helping You Move Through Trauma

CAST Clinical Services is a private organization that provides prompt assessments, referral services, and counselling services throughout Ontario. We specialize in trauma, addiction, and psychotherapy — serving clients in-person and virtually across the province.

We are listed with ConnexOntario and work within a network of care providers to ensure every client finds the right path forward — whether that means counselling, an assessment, a treatment referral, or executive coaching.

🤝

Personalized Care

Every individual's path is unique. We create treatment plans that reflect your specific situation, goals, and strengths.

🌿

Trauma-Informed

We help you clear past trauma in a safe, non-judgmental space and establish thought patterns that support lasting wellness.

💻

Virtual & In-Person

Sessions available virtually, by telephone, in person, or as a hybrid model — wherever you are in Ontario. We work around your life.

🧭

Indigenous Services

Culturally safe counselling and support for Indigenous and Métis individuals. We are committed to providing healing spaces that respect and include traditional teachings.

Meet the Team

Lynne Castonguay

Lynne is the lead counsellor and owner-operator of Cast Clinical Services. She is from Iroquois Falls, Ontario and offers professional services throughout Ontario. As a helper and facilitator for personal healing from trauma, Lynne works with people across Ontario in-person and through virtual technology — video sessions and telephone counselling.

Lynne received her Masters Degree at Wilfrid Laurier University in Indigenous Field of Studies. She has walked with Elders and Teachers, knowledge keepers and researchers, who gifted her with knowledge and tools, trusting in her to share on her path of being a helper. She respects and includes traditional teachings when appropriate and is respectful of Christian beliefs.

She works with the client's beliefs and is open to all types of personal inclinations and preferences — including atheism, non-denominational, LGBTQ+, Two Spirit, and more. She has a high level of respect for all people and provides safe healing spaces conducive to recovery from trauma for individuals, families, and communities.

She will meet you where you are at and work with your strengths to lead you to healing. She refers to Elders and traditional healers when needed in her work.

A true Northerner, Lynne enjoys fishing and spending time in the bush with her family, friends, and faithful dog Grace. She loves mucking out stalls at her farm and spending time with her many companion farm animals at Firefly Acres.
