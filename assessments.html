What CAST Offers

  • Assessment by video conference. All you need is a device with a camera, or a phone
  • Referral services to treatment centres of your choice
  • Help filling out intake forms. We sit with you and guide you through every step
  • Available upon request

Who Needs This

Anyone seeking entry into an alcohol or drug treatment program requires a Treatment Entry Assessment. It is the gateway to accessing the right level of care. CAST makes the process as smooth and stress-free as possible.

What the SAP Assessment Includes

  • Professional Substance Use Assessment (SAP)
  • Initial assessment report including contextual information and treatment recommendations
  • Follow-up with the employee after treatment completion
  • Follow-up report to the employer including relapse prevention provisions
  • Follow-up testing recommendations

Our Approach

We wear the employer's hat when assessing the employee. Our assessments reflect safe measures, not the employee's opinion of whether they are ready to return. Safety at work is crucial, and our reports are thorough, professional, and defensible.

Return to Work Assessment (SAP)

CAST Clinical Services provides each organization (accountable for employee health and safety) with a model that meets its specific risk management needs. We custom-tailor a comprehensive, professional SAP service for your workplace.

Safety at work is crucial. Our Return to Work Assessment is a Substance Abuse Professional (SAP) assessment designed to determine whether an employee can safely return to their duties following a substance-related incident or leave.
