Welcome

A Private Space
for Your Healing

We are a private clinical practice specializing in trauma, addiction, and psychotherapy. We offer counselling, assessments, and healing support to individuals throughout Ontario.

Each individual's path is unique. Creating balance (whatever that means to you) is an important part of Wellness. We help you clear past trauma in a safe space and establish new habits and thought patterns that support your goals.

What We Offer

Our Services

Counselling and assessments, delivered with expertise, compassion, and genuine care for your healing journey.

🧠

Individual Counselling

Customized, one-on-one therapy with an expertly trained clinician dedicated to helping you find joy and balance. Counselling paves the path toward personal transformation, giving relief from post-traumatic disorder and healing from distress.

🧩

EMDR Therapy

A well-researched therapy that helps the brain reprocess distressing memories so they lose their emotional charge. Effective for trauma, anxiety, and painful memories. Lynne is a member of EMDRIA and EMDR Canada.

Treatment Entry & Return-to-Work Assessments

CAST Clinical Services provides referral services for treatment program entry and programming support. We offer option choices for the assessment process and assist with referrals and form completion. Safety at work is crucial. Our SAP (Substance Abuse Professional) assessments help ensure safe, supported returns.

Meet the Team

Lead Counsellor & Owner

Lynne Castonguay

M.S.W. · R.S.W.

Lynne is the lead counsellor and owner-operator of Cast Clinical Services. She is from Iroquois Falls, Ontario and offers professional services throughout Ontario. As a helper and facilitator for personal healing from trauma, Lynne works with people across Ontario in person and through virtual technology utilizing the Ontario Telemedicine Network.

A true Northerner, Lynne enjoys fishing and spending time in the bush with her family, friends, and faithful dog Grace. She loves spending time with her many companion farm animals at Firefly Acres.

Indigenous & Cultural Services Available

Culturally safe care for Indigenous and Métis individuals. Coverage depends on your individual plan, and we will do our utmost to help you find the appropriate funded program whenever possible. You're also welcome to contact your insurance provider, NIHB, or the Métis Nation of Ontario directly.
