Counselling

Individual Counselling

Counselling paves the path towards personal transformation, gives relief from post-traumatic disorder, brings healing from distress and suffering, and offers a chance for lasting change. By dealing with your past, the present becomes more peaceful and the future brightens up.

Individual counselling is ideal to get you the customized treatment you need from an expertly trained clinician dedicated to helping you find joy and balance in your life. Our therapists are dedicated to helping you on your journey to recovery.

Cast Clinical Services offers individual therapy virtually, by telephone, or in person, making quality care accessible from anywhere in Ontario.

What Counselling Can Help With

  • Post-traumatic stress and trauma recovery
  • Addiction and substance use challenges
  • Anxiety, depression, and mood concerns
  • Grief, loss, and life transitions
  • Relationship and family difficulties
  • Personal growth and self-understanding
  • Building new habits and thought patterns

How Sessions Work

Sessions are typically 60 minutes, with 120-minute appointments also available. The number of sessions varies. Some clients benefit from as few as 3, while others continue longer depending on their goals and situation. Your counsellor will work with you to determine the right plan.

Session Options

  • Secure video call (from any device)
  • Telephone counselling
  • In-person (Ontario)
  • Hybrid (in-person and virtual)

Who Benefits Most

Virtual counselling is especially helpful for those in rural or remote areas, people with demanding schedules or caregiving responsibilities, individuals with social anxiety, and anyone who finds it easier to open up from the comfort of their own home.

Accessibility

Virtual Counselling

Virtual counselling has many benefits. Many people need specialized counselling services but are unable to leave their home due to children, work, or other responsibilities. Virtual counselling works around your schedule and is far more accessible.

Many people also find it easier to share deeply personal experiences without the pressure of sitting face-to-face with a therapist. Virtual therapy provides a comfortable, private environment that removes many of the barriers to seeking help.

Cast Clinical Services serves clients throughout Ontario and beyond using technology that is simple and easy to access. All you need is a phone, tablet, or computer.

Indigenous & Métis Services

Culturally Safe Care

At CAST Clinical Services, Indigenous and Métis clients are deeply valued. Lynne Castonguay received her Masters Degree at Wilfrid Laurier University in Indigenous Field of Studies. She has walked with Elders, Teachers, knowledge keepers, and researchers who gifted her with knowledge and tools.

She respects and includes traditional teachings when appropriate, and she meets each client where they are, working with their strengths, beliefs, and personal inclinations to lead them toward healing.
