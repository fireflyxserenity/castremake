Counselling

Individual Counselling

Individual counselling provides a space to pause, reflect, and work through the challenges you're facing. Whether you're navigating anxiety, trauma, grief, relationship concerns, life transitions, neurodiversity, or simply feeling overwhelmed, counselling can help you better understand yourself, strengthen coping strategies, and move toward the life you want to create.

Together, we'll identify your goals and build on your strengths in a way that feels practical, collaborative, and tailored to your unique needs.

I am committed to providing compassionate, individualized care that honours your strengths, experiences, and goals.

Cast Clinical Services offers individual therapy virtually, by telephone, or in person, making quality care accessible from anywhere in Ontario.

How Sessions Work

Sessions are typically 60 minutes, with extended appointments available when clinically appropriate. Some people benefit from just a few sessions, while others choose longer-term support. Together, we'll determine an approach that aligns with your goals, preferences, and circumstances.

There is no expectation that you have everything figured out before your first appointment. We'll start where you are.

EMDR Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitization & Reprocessing

EMDR is a well-researched therapy that helps the brain reprocess distressing memories so they lose their emotional charge. Rather than reliving the past, EMDR helps you move through it, easing the symptoms that traumatic experiences can leave behind.

It is gentle, structured, and effective for children and adults of all ages, and it can be delivered virtually or in person. Many clients find relief from trauma, anxiety, and painful memories that talk therapy alone hasn't fully resolved.

Accessibility

Virtual Counselling

Virtual counselling has many benefits. Many people need specialized counselling services but are unable to leave their home due to children, work, or other responsibilities. Virtual counselling works around your schedule and is far more accessible.

Many people also find it easier to share deeply personal experiences without the pressure of sitting face-to-face with a therapist. Virtual therapy provides a comfortable, private environment that removes many of the barriers to seeking help.

Cast Clinical Services serves clients throughout Ontario and beyond using technology that is simple and easy to access. All you need is a phone, tablet, or computer.

Professional Support

One-on-One Clinical Coaching

The people who care for others often carry the heaviest load. Cast Clinical Services offers confidential, one-on-one clinical coaching for workers and mental-health staff who are feeling the weight of their work.

Whether you are facing workplace stress, burnout, or the added pressures that came with COVID-19, these private sessions give you a steady, supportive space to talk things through, recover your footing, and feel re-energized in your role.
